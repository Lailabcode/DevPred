About DevPred

> About DevPred

DevPred is a server developed by Professor Pin-Kuang Lai's group at Stevens Institute of Technology, designed for predicting properties of monoclonal antibodies (mAbs).

Use our tools for:

  • Generating 30 Spatial Properties of An Antibody Solely Based on the Sequence.
  • High/Low Viscosity Classification Solely Based on the Sequence.
  • 12 Commonly Used Biophysical Property Predictions.

> Publications

  • DeepSP: Deep Learning-Based Spatial Properties to Predict Monoclonal Antibody Stability. Comput. Struct. Biotechnol. J. 2024, 23, 2220-2229. Kalejaye, L.; Wu, I.-E.; Terry, T.; Lai, P.-K. [link]
  • Accelerating High-Concentration Monoclonal Antibody Development with Large-scale Viscosity Data and Ensemble Deep Learning.
  • Machine Learning Models for Predicting Monoclonal Antibody Biophysical Properties from Molecular Dynamics Simulations and Deep Learning-based Surface Descriptors.
